Who are we?

We are a dynamic research group based in the Department of Computer Engineering at the University of Peradeniya. Our team comprises faculty members, undergraduate and postgraduate students, and external collaborators, all dedicated to advancing the frontiers of computer vision theory and its real-world applications.

News

Our research has been featured at leading conferences, with several works selected for oral presentations, underscoring their innovation and impact. Highlights include a novel Retinex-based GAN pipeline for enhancing low-light images, presented at MERCON 2020, and a cutting-edge non-contact method for infant sleep apnea detection, shared at ICIIS 2019. From dynamic background segmentation to GPU-accelerated real-time video enhancement, our contributions span a rich spectrum of computer vision and biomedical applications.
